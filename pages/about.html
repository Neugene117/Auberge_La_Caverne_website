About Us

Nurturing Comfort Communities

Auberge La Caverne was born from a passion for hospitality and a deep love for Rwanda's natural beauty. Founded in 2010, our guesthouse has been welcoming travelers from around the world to experience the warmth of Rwandan hospitality combined with modern comfort and convenience.

Our journey is one of continuous improvement and dedication to creating memorable experiences. From our attentive staff to our thoughtfully designed rooms, we believe that exceptional hospitality is the foundation of a great stay.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Hospitality

We believe in genuine hospitality that goes beyond providing rooms and meals. We treat every guest as family, ensuring personalized attention and care.

Sustainability

We are committed to preserving Rwanda's natural beauty for future generations. Our eco-friendly practices and sustainable operations reflect our environmental responsibility.

Community

We actively support the local community through employment, partnerships with local suppliers, and community development initiatives.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service, from cleanliness to customer service, ensuring the highest standards for our guests.

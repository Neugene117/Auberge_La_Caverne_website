Our Double Room at Auberge La Caverne offers a comfortable and spacious accommodation perfect for couples or small families visiting Rwanda. Featuring a double bed, free Wi-Fi, and modern amenities, this room provides a restful retreat after exploring the scenic landscapes and cultural sites of Kigali and beyond.

Auberge La Caverne's Double Room is thoughtfully designed to provide you with the ultimate comfort during your stay in Rwanda. With modern furnishings, a cozy atmosphere, and attention to detail, this room ensures a restful night's sleep after a day of adventure in the beautiful Rwandan countryside.

Enjoy the convenience of complimentary breakfast served daily, high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property, and easy access to our on-site bar for evening relaxation. The room's private balcony offers breathtaking mountain views, making it an ideal spot for unwinding and taking in the serene natural beauty that surrounds our guesthouse.

Our Double Room includes a range of modern facilities to ensure your comfort: en-suite bathroom with hot water, complimentary Wi-Fi, flat-screen TV, private balcony with mountain views, and daily housekeeping service. Additional amenities available throughout the property include our restaurant serving Rwandan and international cuisine, a well-stocked bar, and laundry services.

For special requests or additional services, please contact our concierge team. We offer airport transfers, guided tours of Kigali and nearby attractions, and can arrange transportation for gorilla trekking or other excursions. Children under 12 stay free when sharing with parents, and we provide cribs upon request. Our flexible check-in and check-out times ensure a stress-free experience.

About Us

Auberge La Caverne was born from a passion for hospitality and a deep love for Rwanda's natural beauty. Founded in 2010, our guesthouse has been welcoming travelers from around the world to experience the warmth of Rwandan hospitality combined with modern comfort and convenience.

Our journey is one of continuous improvement and dedication to creating memorable experiences. From our attentive staff to our thoughtfully designed rooms, we believe that exceptional hospitality is the foundation of a great stay.

About Auberge La Caverne

Our Guesthouse
Events

Auberge La Caverne is a charming guesthouse located in the heart of Rwanda's scenic landscapes. Our establishment offers comfortable accommodations with modern amenities while preserving the authentic Rwandan hospitality. Each room is thoughtfully designed to provide comfort and convenience for our guests.

Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or exploring Rwanda's natural wonders, our guesthouse provides the perfect base. Enjoy stunning mountain views, delicious local cuisine, and personalized service that makes you feel right at home.

Discover exciting events at our hotel! From weddings to corporate gatherings, we offer exceptional venues and services to make your special occasions unforgettable. Our experienced team ensures everything runs smoothly.

Whether it's a birthday party, conference, or holiday celebration, our flexible spaces and catering options cater to all your needs. Contact us to plan your next event.

Family-friendly accommodations with special amenities for kids. Enjoy our kids' club, playground, and family suites designed for comfort and fun. We provide cribs, high chairs, and child-safe environments.

Our hotel offers babysitting services, kids' menus, and activities to keep the little ones entertained while parents relax. Perfect for a memorable family vacation.

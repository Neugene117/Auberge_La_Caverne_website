About Us

Auberge La Caverne was born from a passion for hospitality and a deep love for Rwanda's natural beauty. Founded in 2010, our guesthouse has been welcoming travelers from around the world to experience the warmth of Rwandan hospitality combined with modern comfort and convenience.

Our journey is one of continuous improvement and dedication to creating memorable experiences. From our attentive staff to our thoughtfully designed rooms, we believe that exceptional hospitality is the foundation of a great stay.
